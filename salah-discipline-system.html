    📖 User Guide

    How to Use the
    Salah Discipline System

    A complete step-by-step walkthrough of every feature in this 30-day framework.

    Step-by-Step Guide
    ⚙️
    Step 01

    Configure Your Setup

    Set your Fajr sleep time, alarm time, phone location at night, and your two first-morning actions. These are your behavioral anchors — the rules your morning runs on.

    ⚡
    Step 02

    Set Consequences

    Three tracker-driven triggers are fixed: Salah Missed, Jamaat Missed, Delay in Prayer. Edit the action (what you must do) for each. These fire automatically based on your daily log.

    🏆
    Step 03

    Lock In Rewards

    Set milestone rewards for Day 7, 14, 21, and 30. Write the reward name and why you deserve it. Once you click BEGIN, rewards are permanently locked — claimable only through execution.

    🚀
    Step 04

    Begin the System

    Click BEGIN 30-DAY SYSTEM. You'll go to the Daily Tracker. Day 1 starts immediately. The system tracks your progress from this moment forward.

    📿
    Step 05

    Log Every Prayer Daily

    On the Daily tab, use the three toggle buttons per prayer — ✓ Prayed, 🕐 On Time, 👥 Jamaat. Mark Prayed first before On Time or Jamaat activate. Score (0–5) updates live.

    📝
    Step 06

    Complete the Check-In

    Every day: rate your discipline (1–10), describe your weakest moment, identify the trigger, write one non-negotiable rule for tomorrow. Click SAVE DAY or ADVANCE TO NEXT DAY.

    📊
    Step 07

    Weekly Audit (Auto-Popup)

    At Day 7, 14, 21, and 28 a floating audit window appears automatically. Answer honestly whether you applied your consequences. This directly determines if you can claim your reward.

    🔔
    Step 08

    Reward Notifications

    When a reward becomes claimable, a floating window appears at the center of the screen. Click CLAIM YOUR REWARD or dismiss it. You can also claim from the Review tab at any time.

    Consequence Rules Explained
    ⚡
    Fixed Triggers

    How Tracker-Driven Consequences Work

    🚫 SALAH MISSED — fires when any prayer is not marked Prayed.
    👥 JAMAAT MISSED — fires when prayers were prayed but none in congregation.
    ⏰ DELAY IN PRAYER — fires when a prayer is prayed but not marked On Time.

    Triggers are fixed by the tracker. Only the action is customisable. Go to Setup → Consequence Protocol to edit.

    Exporting Your Data
    💾

    JSON + CSV Download

    Click the ↓ download icon in the top navigation bar at any time. Two files download simultaneously — a full JSON backup of all state, and a per-prayer CSV spreadsheet with every day's log, discipline score, weakest moment, trigger, and tomorrow's rule.

    Live System Status
    Core Philosophy
    🧠
    Foundation

    Discipline Over Motivation

    This system is not built on motivation. Motivation is unreliable — it fluctuates with mood, energy, and circumstance. This system is built on pre-committed rules. You make decisions when you are clear-headed so that when the difficult moment arrives, the decision is already made. You do not negotiate. You execute.

    ⚡
    Accountability

    Pain as a Behavioural Signal

    Consequences exist to create friction around failure. Without consequences, missing a prayer has no cost. With consequences, every miss carries a price. Over time, the cost of missing becomes higher than the cost of showing up. That is the mechanism.

    System Mechanics
    📿
    Salah Tracking

    3 Dimensions Per Prayer

    Each of the 5 daily prayers is tracked across Prayed, On Time, and Jamaat. Daily score is based on prayers completed (0–5).

    📅
    30-Day Grid

    Visual Progress Calendar

    Perfect days appear in cyan. Partial days in violet. Missed days in neon red. The progress bar shows your journey position and prayer completion rate.

    📊
    Weekly Audit

    Mandatory Review Every 7 Days

    At Day 7, 14, 21, and 28 the audit popup opens automatically. You review completion %, confirm consequences were applied, identify patterns, and set adjustments.

    🔒
    Reward Gate

    3 Criteria Must All Be Met

    (1) Day threshold reached, (2) ≥90% completion for those days, (3) Weekly audit confirms consequences were honestly applied. Any gate failing = reward stays locked.

    📝
    Check-In System

    Daily 5-Minute Reflection

    Each day requires a discipline score, identification of your weakest moment, naming the trigger, and a non-negotiable rule for tomorrow. You start seeing your own patterns.

    🔁
    Day Detection

    Auto New-Day Check-In

    If you open the app on a new calendar day, it detects the gap and prompts you to complete yesterday before proceeding. No skipping without accountability.
